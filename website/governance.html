This page states how Sovereign NGO is governed, including structure, oversight, and policy decision processes.

Governance Structure

  • Legal entity: Sovereign Technology Initiative (operating name: Sovereign NGO).
  • Sovereign NGO is a mission-first nonprofit initiative focused on public-interest outcomes.
  • The organization is a nonprofit public-benefit corporation.
  • Revenue is used to advance mission delivery and program operations rather than private profit distribution.
  • The organization uses structured internal policies and documented decision processes.
  • High-impact policy and operational decisions are escalated through governance review when needed.
  • We operate lawfully and with integrity in all activities.

Structured Policies and Decision Process

  • Structured policies are maintained in the Policy Register.
  • The documented decision workflow is defined in the Governance Review Process.
  • Decision outcomes and rationale are recorded in the Governance Decision Log.
