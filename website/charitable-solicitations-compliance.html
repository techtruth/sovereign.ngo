Solicitation Standards

  • Solicitation messaging must be truthful, clear, and not misleading or deceptive.
  • Fundraising activities are reviewed for compliance with applicable local and federal requirements.
  • Required registration and renewal filings are tracked with compliance calendars and controls.

Fundraising Oversight

  • Contracts with professional fundraising services are reviewed for fairness, scope, and transparency.
  • Board oversight includes review of fundraising materials, scripts, and donor communications.
  • Records supporting fundraising disclosures and required filings are maintained accurately.
