Protection of Charitable Assets

  • Charitable assets are used only for mission and lawful nonprofit purposes.
  • Major transactions are documented in board minutes with fairness and mission-impact review.

Dissolution and Wind-Down

  • Fiduciary duties continue through dissolution and termination processes.
  • Remaining assets are transferred only in accordance with law and governing documents.
  • Required filings and notices are completed with the appropriate local government offices before final termination.
  • Where mission continuity is possible, merger options are evaluated before dissolution.
