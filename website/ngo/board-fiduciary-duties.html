Back to Policies

Core Duties

  • Duty of good faith: Board members act honestly, follow governing documents, and advance the mission with lawful use of funds.
  • Duty of loyalty: Board members act in the best interests of the organization and avoid personal benefit at the nonprofit's expense.
  • Duty of care: Board members make informed decisions, review financial condition, attend meetings, and exercise prudent oversight.

Operating Expectations

  • Attend board and committee meetings and review materials before voting.
  • Ask questions and use independent judgment on major decisions.
  • Review bylaws, charter, minutes, financials, and required filings on a regular schedule.
  • Report material information that may affect board decisions, subject to legal confidentiality limits.
  • Support controls for accurate books, record retention, and prevention of fraud, waste, and misuse.
