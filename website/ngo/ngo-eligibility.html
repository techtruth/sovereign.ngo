This statement explains how Sovereign NGO meets NGO eligibility expectations for .NGO and .ONG domain use.

Eligibility Criteria Statement

  1. Public interest focus: Our programs are designed to advance public-benefit outcomes and do not promote discrimination or bigotry.
  2. Nonprofit orientation: We are nonprofit-focused and do not distribute profits for private gain.
  3. Limited government influence: Our governance and decision-making are independent of direct government control.
  4. Independent actor: We are not a political party and are not part of a government body.
  5. Active organization: We actively run mission programs and actively maintain our public website and domain-based email services.
  6. Structured organization: We operate under defined internal governance and policy controls.
  7. Lawful conduct: We act within the law and maintain integrity in our operations.
