Transport Services

Direct-help transport coordination for rides to health, shelter, and service appointments.

Main purpose: Reduce missed services by coordinating ride support and recording handoff completion.

Transport Coordination Workflow

1) Person logs in and requests transport support

2) Team dispatches ride support to service destinations

3) Completion and handoff records are issued for continuity

Direct Help Scope

  • Pickup to destination handoff tracking
  • Coordination notes for cross-provider follow-up
