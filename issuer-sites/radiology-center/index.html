Riverstone Radiology Center

Imaging-only portal for referral intake, X-ray results, and ultrasound results.

Main purpose: Accept doctor referrals and issue signed imaging records.

Radiology Workflow

1) Person logs in with their WebID at the radiology provider

2) Person presents a doctor-issued imaging referral

3) Provider staff performs imaging and issues the signed result credential
